  • Government

    Bangladesh is governed by a unitary form of government under constitution where president is the chief executive He has a council of minsters and there 15 a parliament with 300 elected members. The highest judiciary 1s the Supreme Court headed by chief justice.

  • Administration

    The Country is divided into 4 administrative divisions headed by commissioners. Each division's divided into a number of districts and each district into a number of Upa-zilla. There are municipalities for the urban areas.

  • People

    About 85% of more than 105 million population live in 68.000 villages. They are hardworking adapted to farming, fishing, agro and cottage industries. The urban population is also active in mills and factories, offices, and institutions. More than 86% of the people are Muslims adhering to the tenets of Islam. The literacy rate is 24%.

  • Festivals

    The biggest festival is Eid-ul-Fitre followed by Eid-ul-Azha. Other major festivals celebrated are End-e-Miladunnabi and Shab-e-Barat while other communities celebrate their own. Hindus--Durgapuja. Christians --Xmas, Buddhists--Buddha Purnima. Non-religious celebrations are Bangla New Year's Day. Independence Day.
